Connecting Panthers to On & Off-Campus Events

About the Event

byGSUforGSU is a student-led initiative that highlights exciting events happening in and around Georgia State University. From club fairs and concerts to community service opportunities and networking nights — it’s your one-stop hub for everything Panther-related!

The platform was designed to help students easily find events, explore new interests, and connect with others across all GSU campuses. Whether you live on campus or commute from downtown Atlanta, you’ll always know what’s happening next.

This webpage brings together the best of student life — by students, for students.
